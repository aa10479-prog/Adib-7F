About Arsenal

Arsenal Football Club is a historic English club from North London founded in 1886. Known as The Gunners, Arsenal are famous for attacking football, loyal fans, and legendary players.

The Best Team of All Time

Elite Defense: Saliba and Gabriel form one of the best partnerships in Europe.

Set Pieces: Arsenal are dangerous from corners and free kicks.

Tactics: Arteta adapts systems for every opponent.

Youth: Saka, Rice and Ødegaard lead the future.

Best Player Ever – Thierry Henry

Thierry Henry is Arsenal’s greatest ever player. His pace, skill, finishing and leadership made him a legend at the club and one of the best strikers in football history.
